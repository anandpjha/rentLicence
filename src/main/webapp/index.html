How it Works

Simple & easy three step process

1. Provide the basic Owner &Tenant Information

Click on "start creating new agreement" button & fill the basic owner & tenant information. You can contact us to get help from our representative. Draft soft copy agreement will be created online & sent by email for Owner &Tenant verification.

2. Best door step service for you at home

An appointment for biometric registration & Aadhar validation will be scheduled by our representative for your convenience time (any day including weekend). Our representative visits your home on the scheduled day.

3. Delivery of your Registered Agreements

You will get hard copy of registered agreement within a day.
